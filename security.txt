# Security Policy

Contact: contact@amga.co.uk
Expires: 2026-12-31T23:59:59.000Z

## Preferred Reporting Methods
- Email: contact@amga.co.uk (preferred)
- Do not use public issue trackers for security reports

## Scope
This security.txt applies to all services and applications under the amga.co.uk domain.

## Response Time
We aim to respond to all security reports within 48 hours and provide regular updates on the status of reported vulnerabilities.

## Safe Harbor
We consider security research conducted in accordance with this policy to be authorized. We will not initiate legal action against security researchers for security research activities that follow this policy.

## Disclosure Policy
We follow coordinated vulnerability disclosure practices. Please allow us reasonable time to address vulnerabilities before public disclosure.

## Acknowledgments
We maintain a security acknowledgments page to recognise security researchers who help keep our users safe.
